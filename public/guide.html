Tester's Guide

Welcome to an exclusive round of testing #this before is released to the public. Your frequent usage and feedback is vital to the success of #this.

How to Install #this onto your phone

Step 1
Open the App Store on your iPhone and search for "TestFlight", and install.

Step 2
You should have already received an email with subject TestFlight: Brian Vallelunga|204625366 invited you to test #this, open the email and click "Start Testing".

Step 3
Open TestFlight on your phone. You should see #this appear in your list of available apps to test. Don't worry if you don't see the app it can take a few minutes to process and appear.

Step 4
Once #this appears, download it onto your phone, it will appear as a normal app on your phone, but will have a small orange dot to the left of the name.

Step 5
Start Testing! If you encounter an error or a bug, immediately post it on #this Testing Feedback.
